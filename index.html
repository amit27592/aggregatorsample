General T&C

  • A customer from one mobile can purchase up to 6 SJT (Single Journey Tickets) or 6 RJT (Return Journey Tickets)for same “Origin” & “Destination” for travelling in a group and multiple QR Tickets will be issued.
  • A SJT or a RJT is valid for the same business day, i.e. start and end time of working hours of Hyderabad Metro.
  • Mobile should have internet access for generating the QR ticket.
  • For both SJT and RJT, there will be no refund once the ticket is used at entry.
  • A customer can cancel the unused ticket and avail full refund if the ticket is cancelled within the same business day (business operating hours of Hyderabad Metro). Currently, PhonePe does not support partial cancellation of RJT.
  • “Travel Time Limit” (the difference between entry & exit time at fare collection gate) will be:
    1 Same Station: 30 minutes, (except: Ameerpet: 120 mins)
    2 Different Station: 150 minutes
    3 Penalty/fee for overstay is Rs.15/hour maximum up to Rs60 For a detailed and final travel time limit policy and penalties, please refer to https://www.ltmetro.com/penalty-charter/
  • The above terms & conditions are part of the business rules as stated by Hyderabad Metro. PhonePe is not liable for any damages/issues.
